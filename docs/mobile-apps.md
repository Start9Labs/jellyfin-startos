# Mobile Apps

**Contents**

- [Android](#android)
- [iOS](#ios)

**Instructions**

## Android

1. Visit the app store of your choice and download the [Jellyfin Android app](https://jellyfin.org/downloads).

1. Open the app, you will be prompted for a host; paste your preferred interface URL from your Jellyfin service `Services -> Jellyfin -> Interfaces` in this field.

1. Next enter your username and password and tap "Sign In".


## iOS

1. Open the Apple App Store and download the [Jellyfin iOS app](https://apps.apple.com/app/jellyfin-mobile/id1480192618).

1. Open the app, you will be prompted for a server address; paste your preferred interface URL from your Jellyfin service `Services -> Jellyfin -> Interfaces` in this field.

1. Next enter your username and password and tap "Sign In".


**TIP:** If you are getting connection errors, make sure you are connected to the same LAN and that the interface is accessible over your connection method (i.e. Router VPN etc). Also check that no external VPN is not blocking access to your LAN on your device.
